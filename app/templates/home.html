Add to My Movies Go to this page first. You will see a simple input box. Start typing and matching movies from IMDb will be listed. When one or more movies appear that you have seen, press the Add Movie button to add it to your list. After you have added a few, go to the next page.
Display My Movies This is the main Goldkeys Movies page. The page lists all of your movies and some associated information from IMDb. For each movie, you can enter some fields: Date seen, your rating, and your review. The remaining fields come from IMDb and are updated periodically. This is where you can search and sort. Press a button to sort and press it again to reverse the sort. Click on a movie poster, title or cast member to go to that IMDb page.
Settings This is where you can customize Goldkeys Movies for yourself. You can change the width and height of column and add your own custom columns. Three have been added - date, rating and review - and a total of 10 are available. You can also change your account information like login and email.
Update Movies Users with administrative permissions can run a manual update of IMDb information.
Login As User Use Goldkeys movies as if you were logged in as another user.
