Add Movies Go to this page first. Search for movies by title, cast member, crew, or popularity. For each movie listed, you can:
  • Press I Saw This Movie to add it to your list.
  • Press I Want to See This Movie to add it to your watch list of movies.
After you have added a few movies, try out the Display Movies page.
Display Movies This is the main Goldkeys Movies page. It lists your movies seen or on your watch list, and associated information from IMDb. For each movie, you can enter these fields: Date Seen, My Rating, My Review or up to 10 other fields that you define in Settings. The remaining fields come from IMDb and are updated periodically. This is where you can search and sort. Press a button to sort and press it again to reverse the sort. Click on a movie poster or icon to go to that IMDb page. You can also list movies in a series (e.g. Star Wars)
Settings You can customize Goldkeys Movies for your own needs. Include additional columns of information or hide ones that don't interest you. Change the width and height of columns and add your own custom columns. You can also change the account information that you entered when you registered.
Movie Clubs Members of a movie clubs can share their ratings and reviews. Create a new club or join an existing one.
